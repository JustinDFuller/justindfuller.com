```
I inhale,
never ex-

I think,
never make

In,
never out

Only now
I overflow
bursting at the seams

Hesitate
at the moment of opening

What will pour out?
What will burst forth?

But I must

I simply have no choice
lest I grow sluggish
and lazy
on this diet
of perception
without
conception
```
