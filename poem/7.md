```text
What is this feeling
down in my chest?

I'm being gripped
squeezed, twisted

If this is supposed
to be a good life,
how can anyone survive
a bad life?

If this is a rich life
how empty is a poor life?

Why can't I laugh
Why can't I cry
Why does it all feel so empty
```
